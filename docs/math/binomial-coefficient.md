## 二项式

<script type="math/tex; mode=display">
(x+y)^n = \sum_{k=0}^n\binom{n}{k}x^{n-k}y^k
</script>


## 二项式系数

<script type="math/tex; mode=display">
\binom{n}{k} = C_n^k = \frac{n!}{k!(n-k)!}, k=0,1,...n
</script>
